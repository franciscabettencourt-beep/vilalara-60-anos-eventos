Season Highlights 2026

A Season of Celebration

Throughout 2026, Vilalara Grand Hotel marks six decades with a curated programme of unique events — chef’s dinners, memorable nights, an immersive wellness week, and much more, all set against the breathtaking cliffs of the Algarve.

Gastronomy · 23 May 2026

60 Years on the Cliffs

Opening event of the anniversary season

An intimate dinner inspired by the elegance and atmosphere of the 1960s, on the cliffs where Vilalara’s story began. With the presence of Lara Beirão da Veiga, granddaughter of founder George Ansley, each course is accompanied by visual storytelling and photography celebrating the hotel’s history.
